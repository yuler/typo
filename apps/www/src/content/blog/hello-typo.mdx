We’re building **typo** as a small, fast utility: select text anywhere, hit a shortcut, and let AI suggest a better version that pastes right back where you were.

## Why a desktop app?

Browsers and web apps are great, but selection, focus, and paste behavior differ across sites. A native shell (via [Tauri](https://tauri.app/)) lets typo integrate with the OS clipboard and keyboard in a predictable way.
